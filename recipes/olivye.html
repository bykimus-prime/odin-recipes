Salat Olivye / Салат Олів'є

Description

A "salad" that is more like an actual main course. It's full of vegetables and protein, as well as drenched in mayo. Probably the least healthy salad out there, but delicious.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb of ham
  • 3 medium potatoes
  • 15oz sweet peas, drained, or 2 cups thawed frozen peas
  • 5 boiled eggs
  • 5 medium-large pickles
  • 1/3 cup green onion, chopped
  • 1/4 cup fresh dill, chopped
  • 3/4 to 1 cup mayo (to taste)
  • salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. In the same pot, boil whole unpeeled potatoes and carrots for about 30 minutes, or until knife pierces them smoothly. Don’t let them get too soft.
  2. In a separate pot put eggs in salted cold water. Bring to a boil, turn it off and leave it on the same burner with the lid on for 15 minutes (Remember this; it’s how to make the perfect hard-boiled eggs!!) Cool them down in cold water.
  3. Remove the vegetables and eggs from boiling water and allow them to cool to room temperature prior to chopping. Skin the boiled potatoes and carrots with a small knife. It’s easier to skin the carrots if you make a slit down the length of the carrot and skin in a circle around the carrot. OR you can dice raw carrots and potatoes, boil them together for about 12 mins in unsalted water, remove, rinse with cold water, drain well.
  4. Chop ham, potatoes, carrots, pickles, and eggs into equal sizes (pea size).
  5. Mix together potatoes, carrots, pickles, eggs, green onion, dill and mayo. Add more mayo, Salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Fold in the peas last so they aren't crushed.
